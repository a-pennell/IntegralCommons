# Integral Commons

A modular, open-source commons operating system for communities, neighborhoods, cities, and ecological stewards who want to coordinate without surrendering their data, decisions, or sovereignty to a platform.

Integral Commons optimizes for **coherence, care, and regeneration** — not efficiency, engagement, or profit.

## The Six Layers

| Layer | Component | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 · Local coordination | Local Commons | Neighborhood resource sharing, mutual aid, lightweight governance, time credits |
| 2 · Resource flow | Flow Engine | Movement of materials, skills, time, and care credits through the commons |
| 3 · Ecological awareness | EIL | Real-time ecological impact data embedded in decisions |
| 4 · Relational care | CIP | Care relationships that cannot and should not be quantified |
| 5 · Governance | CommonGround / MCS | Participatory decision-making with constitutional accountability |
| 6 · Intelligence | AI layer | Pattern recognition across the system — legibility without surveillance |

## Constitutional Principles

All layers share seven Tier 1 inviolable principles:

1. **Revocability** — All delegations of authority are revocable
2. **Due Process** — Members subject to removal are entitled to transparent process
3. **Commons Protection** — No decision may privatize shared infrastructure or restrict exit rights *(supreme)*
4. **Forkability** — Any community may fork its governance and leave with its data
5. **Holonic Nesting** — Higher-level holons cannot override the sovereignty of lower-level holons
6. **Deliberation** — No decision proceeds without structured deliberation
7. **Framework Accountability** — The constitutional framework is governed by active communities, not the Integral Commons organization

## What This Is Not

- **Not a platform.** Platforms extract network effects. Integral Commons disperses power.
- **Not neutral.** Specific political commitments are documented and contestable.
- **Not a solution to political problems.** Infrastructure for communities to govern themselves — not a substitute for political change.
- **Not capture-proof by accident.** Anti-capture architecture (financial, technical, governance, data, narrative) is designed in from the start.
