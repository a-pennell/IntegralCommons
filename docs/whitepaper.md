# CommonGround White Paper

## Title
CommonGround: A Decentralized Governance System for Collective Intelligence

---

## Abstract
CommonGround is a governance framework enabling decentralized, participatory, and developmentally-aware decision-making.

---

## Problem

- Centralized systems → capture
- Direct democracy → instability
- DAOs → plutocracy + low engagement

---

## Approach

- Revocable delegation
- Structured deliberation
- Subsidiarity
- Federation

---

## Core Innovations

1. Revocability without instability
2. Deliberation-first governance
3. Scope-aware participation
4. Commons-based architecture

---

## System Design

- Protocol + nodes + apps
- Local-first + federated
- Open standards

---

## Governance Model

- Proposals → deliberation → voting
- Quorum + thresholds
- Stability windows

---

## Risks

- Complexity
- Participation fatigue
- Adversarial behavior

---

## Mitigations

- Strong UX
- Subsidiarity
- Structured deliberation

---

## Conclusion

CommonGround enables systems that distribute power, increase collective intelligence, and support shared stewardship.
