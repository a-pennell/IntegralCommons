# Constitutional Rules (v2)

## 1. Bootstrap Principle
Initial governance is established via consent.

## 2. Revocability Principle
All delegations are revocable.

## 3. Bounded Referendum
Referenda require minimum support thresholds.

## 4. Subsidiarity
Decisions occur at the lowest competent level.

## 5. Stability Windows
Decisions cannot be immediately overturned.

## 6. Rate Limiting
Members cannot spam governance actions.

## 7. Deliberation First
All decisions require structured deliberation.

## 8. Due Process
Removal requires transparent process + thresholds.

## 9. Participation Integrity
Quorum and diversity required.

## 10. Commons Protection
No enclosure or privatization of shared infrastructure.

## 11. Forkability
System may be forked at any time.
