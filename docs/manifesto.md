# CommonGround Manifesto

We believe:

That systems ignoring human development create fragmentation
That centralized power leads to capture
That democracy without structure becomes noise
That structure without participation becomes domination

We reject:

- Extractive platforms
- Opaque governance
- Irreversible power

We affirm:

- Stewardship over ownership
- Revocability of power
- Transparency of process
- Plurality of perspectives

We commit to building systems that:

- Increase awareness
- Support meaningful disagreement
- Enable local governance
- Remain open and forkable

We understand:

No system guarantees wisdom
But systems can enable or constrain it

CommonGround exists to enable it.
