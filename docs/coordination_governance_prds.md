## 1. Project: "Synapse" (Democratic Planning Engine)

### 1.1 Overview
Synapse is a resource-matching algorithm designed to replace the chaotic price signals of capitalism with a real-time, data-driven map of human needs and ecological capacity. It facilitates "Participatory Economics" (Parecon) at scale.

### 1.2 User Personas
- **The Household:** Wants to ensure they have food, energy, and essentials without navigating predatory markets.
- **The Production Collective:** Needs to know what to produce, in what quantity, and where the raw materials are coming from.

### 1.3 Functional Requirements
- **Need-Input API:** A privacy-preserving interface for users to log "Standard Consumption Bundles."
- **Ecological Guardrail Engine:** A backend service that cross-references production plans against local biodiversity and carbon thresholds.
- **The Matchmaker:** A multi-objective optimization algorithm that prioritizes "highest social need" and "lowest ecological impact."

### 1.4 Technical Stack (Proposed)
- **Engine:** Rust (for performance and safety).
- **Optimization:** Linear Programming (LP) and Heuristic Search.
- **Privacy:** Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP) to ensure aggregate needs are known without exposing individual household habits.

## 2. Project: "FlowState" (Liquid Democracy Platform)

### 2.1 Overview
FlowState moves beyond the "4-year election cycle" by allowing citizens to delegate their voting power to trusted experts or peers on a per-topic basis, with the ability to revoke it instantly.

### 2.2 Key Features
- **The Delegation Graph:** A visual representation of how your vote is traveling. If you delegate to 'Jane' for Ecology, you can see if 'Jane' has further delegated that power to a specific NGO.
- **Topic-Based Tagging:** Users can set different proxies for #Climate, #Education, and #Infrastructure.
- **Proposal Versioning:** A Git-like system where laws are "pulled," "forked," and "merged" based on community consensus.

### 2.3 Success Metrics
- **Representation Density:** Measuring the percentage of the population actively represented in any given vote.
- **Delegation Velocity:** The speed at which users reassign votes in response to proxy performance.

## 3. Project: "Co-Op OS" (Platform Coop Framework)

### 3.1 Overview
An open-source infrastructure suite that allows any group of workers to launch their own peer-to-peer service (like ride-hailing or delivery) without a corporate middleman.

### 3.2 Core Modules
- **Governance Module:** Tools for quadratic voting on company policy and executive (steward) selection.
- **Automated Treasury:** A smart-contract-based wallet that handles "Surplus Value Distribution."
- **Reputation Portability:** An identity layer that allows a worker to bring their 5-star rating from one coop to another.

### 3.3 Security & Compliance
- **Sybil Resistance:** Proof-of-personhood protocols to ensure one worker = one vote.
- **Local Legal Wrappers:** Pre-built legal templates for registering the entity as a worker cooperative in various jurisdictions.
