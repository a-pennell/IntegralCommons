## 4. Project: "Equip" (Digital Library of Things)

### 4.1 Overview
Equip is a hyper-local asset-sharing platform designed to shift the paradigm from "Ownership" to "Access." It manages the lifecycle of community-held physical goods.

### 4.2 Functional Requirements
- **IoT Integration:** Smart locks and lockers that unlock when a user checks out an item via the app.
- **Durability Tracking:** A ledger that records hours of use, torque applied, or wear-and-tear metrics to predict when maintenance is needed.
- **Community Insurance Fund:** A micro-contribution model where every "borrow" adds a tiny amount to a collective repair fund.

### 4.3 User Experience
- **Map View:** Find the nearest industrial drill or bread maker within a 5-minute walk.
- **Instruction Hub:** AR-overlay tutorials for every item to ensure safe and proper usage.

## 5. Project: "WikiForge" (Open-Source Hardware Repo)

### 5.1 Overview
WikiForge is the "GitHub for the Physical World." It hosts modular, repairable, and locally manufacturable designs for the essential technologies of life.

### 5.2 Repository Standards
- **BOM (Bill of Materials):** Must prioritize recycled or locally abundant materials.
- **Tool-Agnosticism:** Designs must be categorized by the tools required (e.g., "Hand tools only" vs. "CNC/3D-Print required").
- **The "Right to Repair" Scorecard:** Every design is rated on how easy it is to disassemble and fix.

### 5.3 Collaboration Tools
- **Branching Blueprints:** If someone adapts a water pump for high-salinity environments, they "branch" the design for others in coastal regions.

## 6. Project: "Kindred" (Tokenized Care Credits)

### 6.1 Overview
Kindred creates a "Care Economy" ledger. It quantifies social contributions that the market ignores, such as elder care, mentoring, and emotional labor.

### 6.2 The "Proof of Care" Mechanism
- **Mutual Attestation:** When a care act is performed, both the provider and receiver (or a witness) sign the transaction.
- **Time-Banking:** 1 hour of care = 1 credit, regardless of the "market value" of the specific task, ensuring radical equality.

### 6.3 Economic Integration
- **The Flourishing Floor:** These credits can be redeemed for "premium" community assets or used as social proof to access leadership roles within the "Co-Op OS" ecosystem.
